FAQ

What is Nature’s Platter?

Nature’s Platter is an e-commerce platform specializing in fresh, organic, and natural food products. We offer a variety of groceries, fruits, vegetables, dairy products, and eco-friendly essentials, ensuring high-quality and sustainable shopping for our customers.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is placed, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also track your order status by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section.

What is your return and refund policy?

We offer a hassle-free return policy for damaged or incorrect items. If you receive a defective product, please contact our customer support within 7 days of delivery. Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after the returned item is inspected.
